About TaskWay

TaskWay is the most motivating way to get things done. Instead of another grey checklist, a cheerful leaf buddy coaches you out loud, your real voice runs it hands-free, and focus music keeps you in the zone.

  • 🌿 A talking buddy that cheers you on through every task
  • 🎙️ Hands-free voice control — just say “done” or “skip”
  • 🗂️ Your day in clean thirds: one-time, overdue, schedule
  • 🎵 Built-in looping focus music
  • 🧳 Photo-based smart packing lists
  • ⏱️ Gentle countdown rings that resume where you left off
